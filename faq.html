What services does your dental clinic in New Delhi offer?

Our dental clinic in New Delhi offers a comprehensive range of services, including routine check-ups, cleanings, fillings, root canals, teeth whitening, orthodontics, and emergency dental care. How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?

It is generally recommended to visit the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, if you have specific dental issues or concerns, more frequent visits may be necessary. Our team will provide personalized recommendations based on your oral health needs.

03

Do you offer emergency dental services in New Delhi?

Yes, we provide emergency dental services in New Delhi. Whether you have a severe toothache, a broken tooth, or any other dental emergency, our team is here to help. Contact us immediately, and we will schedule an appointment as soon as possible.

What should I expect during my first visit to your clinic?

During your first visit, we will conduct a thorough examination of your teeth and gums, including X-rays if necessary. We will discuss your dental history, any concerns you may have, and create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs. Our goal is to ensure you feel comfortable and informed throughout the process.

Do you accept insurance, and what payment options are available?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will assist you in understanding your coverage and filing claims. Additionally, we offer flexible payment options, including cash, credit/debit cards, and EMI facilities, to ensure that quality dental care is accessible to everyone.
